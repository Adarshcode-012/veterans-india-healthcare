Recognition & Rewards

Celebrating healthcare heroes and honoring exceptional service to veterans and communities across India.

Healthcare Hero Awards

Recognizing exceptional contributions to veteran healthcare and community service

1st

Healthcare Hero Gold Award

The highest honor for exceptional healthcare professionals who have demonstrated outstanding commitment to veteran care and community health.

  • Exemplary service record
  • Innovation in healthcare delivery
  • Community impact
2nd

Veteran Health Champion

Recognizing active medical volunteers who have made significant contributions to veteran wellness programs.

  • Consistent volunteer service
  • Specialized veteran care
  • Program development
3rd

Community Health Partner

Honoring hospitals, clinics, and NGOs that have partnered with us to deliver quality healthcare services.

  • Partnership excellence
  • Resource contribution
  • Sustainable impact

Our Journey of Recognition

Key milestones in our recognition and awards program

2023 - First Healthcare Hero Awards

Launched our inaugural awards program recognizing 50 healthcare professionals across India.

2022 - Partnership Recognition Program

Established formal recognition for hospitals and NGOs partnering with our healthcare initiatives.

2021 - Volunteer Certification Launch

Introduced professional certification programs for healthcare volunteers and coordinators.

2020 - Community Health Awards

Recognized outstanding contributions to rural healthcare and veteran wellness programs.
